\chapter{Non-Functional Requirements}
\label{chap:non-functional-requirements}

This chapter specifies the non-functional requirements of the EcoTrace
Intelligent Electronic Waste Recovery and Circular Economy Management
System.

Non-functional requirements define the quality attributes, operational
constraints, security expectations, performance characteristics, and
other system properties that influence the overall effectiveness,
reliability, and maintainability of the platform.

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:nfr-introduction}

The EcoTrace platform shall satisfy all specified quality requirements
throughout its operational lifecycle.

The non-functional requirements specified in this chapter shall guide
system implementation, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
