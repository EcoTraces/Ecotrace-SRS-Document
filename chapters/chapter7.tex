\chapter{Business Rules}
\label{chap:business-rules}

This chapter specifies the business rules that govern the operation of
the EcoTrace Intelligent Electronic Waste Recovery and Circular Economy
Management System.

Business rules define the policies, constraints, validations, and
operational procedures that the system shall enforce during normal
operation.

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:business-rules-introduction}

The business rules described in this chapter ensure that electronic
waste management activities are performed consistently, securely, and
according to the objectives of the EcoTrace platform.

These rules apply to all authorized users and system modules.

\section{User Registration Rules}
\label{sec:user-registration-rules}

\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{10cm}|}

\hline
\textbf{Rule ID} &
\textbf{Business Rule}\\
\hline
\endfirsthead

\hline
\textbf{Rule ID} &
\textbf{Business Rule}\\
\hline
\endhead

BR-REG-001 &
Every user shall register using a unique email address.\\
\hline

BR-REG-002 &
Each user account shall be assigned exactly one primary role.\\
\hline

BR-REG-003 &
Required registration fields shall be completed before account creation.\\
\hline

BR-REG-004 &
Only verified accounts shall access protected resources.\\
\hline

BR-REG-005 &
Duplicate user accounts shall not be permitted.\\
\hline

\end{longtable}

\hline
\endfirsthead

\hline
\textbf{Rule ID} &
\textbf{Business Rule}\\
\hline
\endhead

BR-AUTH-001 &
Users shall authenticate before accessing protected modules.\\
\hline

BR-AUTH-002 &
Only active user accounts shall be permitted to log in.\\
\hline

BR-AUTH-003 &
Expired sessions shall be terminated automatically.\\
\hline

BR-AUTH-004 &
Administrative privileges shall only be granted to authorized personnel.\\

BR-AUTH-005 &

\section{Electronic Waste Registration Rules}
\label{sec:waste-registration-rules}

\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{10cm}|}

\hline
\textbf{Rule ID} &
\textbf{Business Rule}\\
\hline
\endfirsthead

\hline
\textbf{Rule ID} &
\textbf{Business Rule}\\
\hline
\endhead

BR-EWR-001 &
Each electronic waste item shall receive a unique identification number.\\
\hline

BR-EWR-002 &
Waste registration shall include the waste category.\\
\hline

BR-EWR-003 &
At least one image shall be uploaded during registration.\\
\hline

BR-EWR-004 &
The system shall record the registration date and time.\\
\hline

BR-EWR-005 &
GPS location shall be recorded when available.\\
\hline

\end{longtable}
